How to Play

  • Objective: Navigate from the green start point to the red finish point without hitting walls!

Game Phases:

  • - Memorizing Phase (10s): Study the maze layout with all walls visible
  • - Moving Phase (20s): Navigate to the finish while walls are hidden

Controls:

  • - Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • - Hint Button: Reveal walls for 1 second (once per stage)

Rules:

  • - You start with 5 lives
  • - Hitting a wall or running out of time costs 1 life
  • - Each stage gets progressively harder
  • - Game ends when you lose all lives

Tip: Use the memorizing phase wisely to plan your route!
